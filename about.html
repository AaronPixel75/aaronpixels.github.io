About Impact High School

Impact High School is a premier educational institution dedicated to nurturing the leaders of tomorrow. Founded in 2005, our school has consistently provided a holistic learning environment that combines academic excellence with character development. Located in the heart of Buikwe District, Lugazi, we offer state-of-the-art facilities, a diverse curriculum, and a vibrant community that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration.

Our mission is to empower students to achieve their full potential by providing a supportive and inclusive environment that encourages intellectual curiosity, personal growth, and social responsibility. With a team of highly qualified educators and a commitment to innovation, we strive to prepare our students for success in a rapidly changing world.
